Fried Egg

Description

A delicious, nutrient-filled and natural oiled-up egg, fried over 100 years of human technological advances (Stove and non-stick pan)

Ingredients

  • 1 (and only one) egg, optionally fried
  • Handful of succulent oil
  • Cooking vessel

Steps

  1. Heat some of that scrupalocius oil in a pan
  2. Crack and drop the egg innards in the searing oil
  3. Wait anywhere from 1 minute to the heat death of the Universe
  4. Serve and enjoy!
